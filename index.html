Our Heritage

The Saaoui & Cie Story

Bridging the gap between heavy industrial manufacturing and family-led craftsmanship, Saaoui & Cie has evolved from traditional stone block processing in Fenaia Ilmaten to pioneering complex GRP and resin-stone sanitary fixtures.

Our facility along the RN 26 in Avadou maintains the highest manufacturing standards with high-tonnage molding presses and a strictly wheelchair-accessible layout, ensuring both precision and inclusivity in our operations.

2009
Founded as a traditional stone processing workshop in Fenaia Ilmaten
2014
Expanded into GRP composite manufacturing with industrial-scale presses
2018
Launched premium resin-stone sanitary line serving contractors across Bejaia
2023
Modernized facility on RN 26 with full accessibility compliance and expanded B2B operations

Behind the Craft

Technical Innovation & B2B Portal

Engineered for architects, interior designers, real estate developers, and civil contractors requiring strict material specifications.

Material Science

Our engineered resin-stone composite combines natural mineral powder with high-grade resin matrices to produce surfaces demonstrably stronger and more stain-resistant than standard natural stone. The molecular bonding process ensures thermal shock resistance up to 180 degrees C and complete non-porosity.
